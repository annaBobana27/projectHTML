Anna's Top 5 Favorite Food

Welcome to Anna's ranking of her favorite food. These rankings will consist of dishes from cultures, restaurants, and fast food joints. Each item will also include a list of reasons why it deserves that ranking.

  1. Kimchi Fried Rice
    • Why is it the best?
      • the right amount of spice
      • Pairs well with everything
      • Has carbs, protein, vegetables; it's the full package
  2. Spicy Chicken Sandwich Chick Fil A Meal
    • What about it is so good?
      • The spiciness level is 10/10
      • Chick Fil A sauce with the sandwich makes it even better
      • Waffle fries are the best fries
    • What can it improve?
      • Sometimes it just doesn't hit the spot
      • Chicken can be just a little too spicy at times
  3. Sushi
    • What makes sushi buss?
      • Refreshing
      • Lots of variety to choose from
      • AYCE places give me a bang for my buck
    • What makes it 3rd on the list?
      • Sometimes I'm just not in a fishy mood
      • The avocado is an ick to me
      • The texture can just be a little too much that I need a break
  4. Chicken Wings
    • What makes wings delish?
      • It's a comfort food, gets be back up when life knocks me down
      • When you dip it into ranch, it gives it a little zest to it.
    • How come chicken wings are only 4?
      • Sometimes, I don't want to get my hands all dirty and saucy
      • They're not filling enough
      • They break the bank a lot
  5. California Burrito from Roberto's Tacos
    • How does this burrito compare to any other?
      • It's got all the fillings: Pico De Gallo, Queso, Crema, Fries, Carne Asada
      • The red and green salsa cannot compare
      • It's warm and comforting, like holding a little baby
    • If it's so good, why's it the last item?
      • At times, I feel gross when I eat it after
      • It's not good when you reheat it, so you have to eat it all in one sitting which is crazy
      • They raised the prices which is absolutely absurd
